General Information

Amateur Computer Group of New Jersey (ACGNJ) is a computer user group based in Scotch Plains, NJ. Founded in May of 1975, we are the oldest computer club still in operation.

The club is comprised of a number of special interest groups ( SIGs ) which have meetings throughout the month.

We have been a primary sponsor and contributor to the annual Trenton Computer Festival (TCF) since its inception in 1976.

Speakers Wanted!

If you have or want a specific or general topic of interest please consider giving an informal talk at an upcoming meeting, or suggesting to the Planning Committee the topic or subject for the talk or suggested speaker.
