About the Trenton Computer Festival (TCF)

ACGNJ has been proud to be a major sponsor of TCF since its inception in 1976. Our members have donated their time and talent to TCF as speakers and volunteers. Please visit the TCF website for more detailed information.

Also, don't forget to follow TCF on and .

TCF 2020

TCF 2020 will be held on Saturday, March 21. The keynote speaker will be Jerry Foster, Co-Founder and CTO at Plex Systems. There were over 50 talks, workshops, tutorials, demos, and special events including:

  • Al Katz, Hank Kee and Don Libes will facilitate a special session on remembering Sol Libes, founder of ACGNJ.
  • Lou Judice will facilitate a boot camp on WordPress.
  • Randall Cole will present a talk on Machine Learning on Microsoft Azure.
  • Professor Barry Burd will present talks on Flutter and Quantum Computing.
  • Bill Brutzman will present a talk on Cloud AI with Amazon Web Services.
  • Michael Redlich and Chuck Knight will present talks on Java and Python, respectively, in the Object-Oriented Programming University track.
  • On-line Mystabar gaming.
  • Ham Cram Session & Exam: earn an Amateur Radio License in one day at TCF!
  • ...and much more!

Please visit the website for more details!

Please note that the TCF IT Professional Conference, originally scheduled for Friday, March 20, was canceled this year, but will return in 2021.

TCF 2019

TCF 2019 was held on Saturday, March 23 at The College of New Jersey, Ewing, New Jersey. This year' keynote speaker was Tony Sager, Senior Vice President and Chief Evangelist at The Center for Internet Security (CIS). There were over 50 talks, workshops, tutorials, demos, and special events including:

  • Professor Barry Burd presented talks on Flutter, Quantum Computing, and Blockchain.
  • Lou Judice facilitated a boot camp on WordPress.
  • Michael Redlich and Chuck Knight presented talks on Java and Python, respectively, in the restructured Object-Oriented Programming University track.
  • Bill Brutzman presented a talk on Raspberry Pi and IOT.
  • On-line Mystabar gaming.
  • Ham Cram Session & Exam: earn an Amateur Radio License in one day at TCF!
  • ...and much more!

Also, the TCF IT Professional Conference was held on Friday, March 22.

TCF 2018

TCF 2018 was held on Saturday, March 17 at The College of New Jersey, Ewing, New Jersey. This year's keynote speaker was Dr. Nick van Terheyden, Founder & CEO at Incremental Healthcare. There were over 50 talks, workshops, tutorials, demos, and special events including:

  • David Soll presented talks on Windows 10 and Microsoft Office.
  • Professor Barry Burd presented talks on app development, Quantum Computing, and Alexa skills.
  • Michael Redlich presented talks on object-oriented programming, C++, and Java.
  • High school student, Sam Zeloof, demonstrated how to make semiconductors and IC chips at home.
  • Drone/Quadcopter/Maker Demos
  • Ham Cram Session & Exam: earn an Amateur Radio License in one day at TCF!
  • ...and much more!

Also, the TCF IT Professional Conference was held on Friday, March 16.

TCF 2017

TCF 2017 was held on Saturday, March 18 at The College of New Jersey, Ewing, New Jersey. This year's keynote speaker was Professor Ragunathan "Raj" Rajkumar from Carnegie Mellon University. This year's featured speaker was Dr. Greg Olsen speaking on his experiences driving a Tesla with autonomous control. There were over 50 talks, workshops, tutorials, demos, and special events including:

  • David Soll presented talks on Windows 10 and Microsoft Office.
  • Professor Barry Burd presented talks on Bluetooth Beacons, Functional Programming, and Amazon Echo.
  • Michael Redlich presented talks on object-oriented programming, C++, and Java.
  • Microsoft presentations and special workshop on “Safe Computing”
  • Drone/Quadcopter/Maker Demos
  • Ham Cram Session & Exam: earn an Amateur Radio License in one day at TCF!
  • ...and much more!

Also, the TCF IT Professional Conference was held on Friday, March 17.
