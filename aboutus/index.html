Who We Are

Established in 1975, the Amateur Computer Group of New Jersey (ACGNJ) is the oldest continuously running computer club in the United States. We are a recognized non-profit 501(c)(3) educational group. The club consists of a number of Special Interest Groups (SIGs) which meet at various times during the month. These meetings are open to the public and address a variety of proficiency levels. Every spring, ACGNJ sponsors the annual Trenton Computer Festival (TCF), an all-day Saturday event consisting of a vendors, seminars, and workshops.

Meetings

We currently hold all meetings on-line mostly using Jitsi.
Some SIG Leaders may choose to use other platforms for their meetings.

All in person meetings are held at the Scotch Plains Rescue Squad, second floor unless otherwise noted.

Code of Conduct

We value the participation of our club members and guests and we strive for an enjoyable and fulfilling experience. Accordingly, all club members and guests are expected to conform to our formal Code of Conduct at all club-related events.

Planning Meeting

Every year, the Board of Directors facilitates a Planning Meeting to brainstorm topics for the Main and Window Pains meetings. The Planning Meeting is usually held during the June Random Access meeting, i.e., the third Friday of June. All members in good standing are invited to participate in this annual event.

The topics for the current season can be found here.

Meeting Cancelation

In case of inclement weather, the following protocol will be used for canceling in person meetings:

  • Each SIG Leader shall be responsible for making the decision to cancel their respective SIG meeting.
  • The President shall be responsible for making the decision to cancel the Main Meeting and Random Access.
  • Weather conditions will be considered for the start and end of the meeting time.
  • Ideally, the decision to cancel should be made by 4:30 PM ET.
  • Every effort will be made to notify club members as soon as the decision is made to cancel. This includes e-mail and updates to the website and Meetup page.

Board of Directors

The purpose of the Board of Director meetings is to discuss and decide upon issues affecting the club in its normal business activities. All decisions are based upon the ACGNJ Constitution and By-Laws and in accordance with our 501(c)(3) status. Meetings are held on Jitsi the Tuesday prior to the Main Meeting usually starting at 7:00 PM ET.

All members are welcome to attend these meetings and may request to voice their opinion about subjects on the agenda. Only directly elected officers and directors may vote on issues.

Officer and director responsibilities are explained in detail on this page. The Board of Directors may, due to security and/or privacy issues, limit membership participation on these issues. The Chairpersons for each Special Interest Group (SIG) or their vice-chairs are requested to attend all Board of Director meetings. The duties and responsibilities of SIG chairpersons are explained in detail on this page.
